Introduction to Among Us

Among Us is a wildly popular online multiplayer game of deception, teamwork, and deduction. Crewmates must complete tasks while an Impostor secretly sabotages the ship and eliminates players. Communication, suspicion, and smart decisions decide the outcome of each round.

How to Play Among Us

As a Crewmate, finish your tasks and watch for suspicious behavior. Report bodies, call meetings, and vote out the Impostor. As an Impostor, blend in, sabotage critical systems, and eliminate players without getting caught.

Tips and Strategies

  • As Crewmate: Stick with groups and confirm visual tasks.
  • As Impostor: Sabotage often to create chaos and isolate targets.
