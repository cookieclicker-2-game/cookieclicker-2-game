Introduction to Idle Planet Gym Tycoon

Idle Planet Gym Tycoon is a creative idle management game where you open gyms on different planets and train alien customers. From tiny space stations to huge intergalactic complexes, your goal is to make every visitor stronger while growing your profit.

How to Play Idle Planet Gym Tycoon

Start with a small gym on one planet and a few basic machines. As alien clients arrive, they pay for workouts and services. Use your income to unlock new machines, hire trainers, expand rooms, and eventually open gyms on new planets with higher-paying customers.

Tips and Strategies

  • Invest in key equipment upgrades first to increase client capacity and workout speed.
  • Unlock new planets as soon as your current planet is stable to access better income tiers.
  • Keep balancing staff, machines, and upgrades so there are no big bottlenecks in your gym flow.
