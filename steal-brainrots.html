Introduction to Steal Brainrots

Steal Brainrots is a fast and chaotic meme-flavored action game where you sprint through wild levels collecting glowing brainrot tokens. Each run is packed with obstacles, moving hazards, and funny visual chaos that keeps every attempt fresh.

How to Play Steal Brainrots
