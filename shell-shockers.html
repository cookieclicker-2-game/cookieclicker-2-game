Introduction to Shell Shockers

Shell Shockers is a unique first-person shooter where every player is an armed egg. Choose your favorite egg soldier, grab a powerful yolk-filled weapon, and jump into intense online battles against players from around the world.

How to Play Shell Shockers

Use your keyboard and mouse to move, aim, and shoot. Each weapon has a different playstyle, from long-range sniping to close-quarters shotguns. Watch your ammo, keep moving, and use cover to protect your fragile shell from cracking.
