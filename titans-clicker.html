Introduction to Titans Clicker

Titans Clicker is a fast-paced idle clicker game where every tap sends waves of damage into towering titan bosses. Watch massive health bars melt as you recruit new heroes, unlock damage multipliers, and build a team that never stops attacking.

How to Play Titans Clicker

Click on the titan to deal damage, then spend your gold on new heroes and upgrades. Each hero attacks automatically, so the longer you play the more damage you deal even without clicking. Defeat one titan to move on to the next, with higher health and better rewards.

Tips and Strategies

  • Upgrade your top-performing heroes first to boost DPS efficiently.
  • Use active clicking during boss fights to push through tight time limits.
  • Revisit earlier stages to farm gold if you are stuck on a tough titan.

Similar Games You Can Try
