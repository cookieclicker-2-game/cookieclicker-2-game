Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use CookieGames, a static website hosted on GitHub Pages.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not require user accounts, logins, or personal submissions.

Cookies

CookieGames does not use tracking cookies. However, some embedded games hosted externally may use cookies as part of their operation. These cookies are controlled by the respective game publishers, not by CookieGames.

Third-Party Content

Some pages contain iframe embeds of online games. These third-party hosts may collect usage data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control or access that data.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) only to understand site traffic. GA4 logs general, non-personal browsing data such as pages visited, device type, and session duration. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy over time. Continued use of the site means you accept any updated version.
