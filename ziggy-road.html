Introduction to Ziggy Road

Ziggy Road is an endless road-crossing arcade game where every move must be carefully timed. Dash across busy streets, dodge traffic, avoid obstacles, and try not to get hit as you zig-zag your way through danger.

How to Play Ziggy Road

Tap or click to move forward and sometimes sideways depending on the control scheme. Watch out for cars, trucks, trains, and other obstacles that appear without warning. One wrong step and the run is over.
